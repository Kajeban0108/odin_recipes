Lasagna

Description

This will be a recipe showing you how to make a lasagna

This will be an Excellent and Delicious experience and I hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatos
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps:

  1. Mash the Garlic
  2. Add Tomato Paste
  3. Mix Everything Together
  4. Chuch it in the Oven
  5. Enjoy!
