Cereal

Description

This will be a recipe showing you how to make your Morning Cereal

This will be an Excellent and Delicious experience and I hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • Kellogs Frosties!
  • Milk
  • Spoon
  • Bowl
  • You!

Steps:

  1. Get an empty clean bowl
  2. Pour a good amount of cereal into it
  3. Pour Milk into the bowl with the cereal. (Not the Other Way Around)
  4. Get your spoon and dig it!
  5. Enjoy!
